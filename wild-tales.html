Description

Absolutely fantastic Argentinian omnibus movie that shows the absurdity and pointlessness of life, and explores the extremities of human behavior when put in distressing situations. The movie brilliantly succeeds in encapsulating and humoristically deconstructing situations spanning from simple everyday plights like dealing with parking tickets to serious issues like death, murder and love. The characters of the film, vulnerable in the face of a reality that shifts and suddenly turns unpredictable, cross the thin line that divides civilization and barbarism.

What it makes for, is a seriously hilarious and charmingly deranged flick that is quick to grow on you. A rollercoaster of a movie that's accompanied by exceptional soundtrack by Gustavo Santaolalla.
